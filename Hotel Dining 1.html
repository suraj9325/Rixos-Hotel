INDIAN



The Colosseum. The Leaning Tower of Pisa. Leonardo da Vinci. Galileo. Machiavelli. Columbus. Pizza. Wine. Pasta.
What do all these remind you of? Undoubtedly, of the country called Italy! Italy is famous for its architecture, literature, music, theatre,
paintings, cinema, fashion, etc. But the most popular thing about Italy is its cuisine – the food.
Now, we all know about pasta and pizzas. But Italy is a lot more than these!
It is about cheeses, wines, coffee, and much more. Italy is a food lover’s paradise. And that is why food lovers flock to Italy.
