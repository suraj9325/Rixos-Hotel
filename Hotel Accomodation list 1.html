DELUXE DOUBLE/TWIN ROOMS

The suites have an exquisitely appointed bedroom and an adjoining living room. The Deluxe rooms have sloping roofs and wooden walls showcasing the design elements
that surrounded royalty of yore. These rooms are elegantly appointed with Burma teak flooring and have king beds. The bathrooms include a vanity counter, a bathtub and
come with Elgin designer toiletries.


Room features

  •   32 inch Sony LCD Television & Satellite Television

  •   Direct dial telephone in bedroom & bathroom

  •   Free wireless internet in all bedrooms

  •   Attached private bath with shower,& bath slippers

  • Fireplaces on request in selective rooms
